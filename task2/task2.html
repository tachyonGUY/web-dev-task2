Netaji Subhas University of Technology

About Us

Established in 1983, Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology (NSIT) was granted university status to become Netaji Subhas University of Technology. According to the NIRF rankings 2023, NSUT Delhi is rated 60th in the engineering category and 95th in the university category. The main campus of NSIT, a state institution, is situated in Dwarka, Delhi on 145 acres of land. NSIT consists of 13 departments, and they provide 11 undergraduate, 15 graduate, and 11 doctorate programs in diverse fields.
The popular programs offered by NSIT are BTech, MTech, MBA, and BBA courses. NSUT BTech Admissions are based on JEE Main Cutoff and seat allocation is done via JAC Delhi counselling. The total cost of the BTech fees for NSIT is INR 3.5 Lakhs. The BTech HS closing cutoff is around 13805, while OS is 6466. During the 2022 placements, 826 BTech and 151 MTech students were placed. The median package provided to BTech and MTech students was INR 16 LPA and INR 14 LPA, respectively. Amazon, Microsoft, Honeywell, Google, IBM, Accenture, Honda, HCL, Honda, Birlasoft, and many more are among the top employers.
